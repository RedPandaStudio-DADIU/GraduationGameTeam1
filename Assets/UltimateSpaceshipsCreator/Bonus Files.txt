Additional 4K textures for the smaller spaceships, texture color masks and PSD files are available for free download from the Bonus Files located here: http://u3d.as/2Abs

When importing the additional bonus files, it's recommended that you start a new project, download and import the bonus files to it. Then manually replace the 2K textures you wish to change with the new 4K textures.

For more info please check the documentation or contact us.
